Game Guide

🎯 Basic Gameplay

  • Click the Luci gif to contribute to the global counter
  • Watch for the golden bunbat for bonus points!
  • Your country's total is shown in the rankings
  • Earn tier badges based on your click count: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Legendary

🏆 Tier System

  • 🥉 Bronze: Less than 1,000 clicks
  • 🥈 Silver: 1,000 - 9,999 clicks
  • 🥇 Gold: 10,000 - 99,999 clicks
  • 👑 Legendary: 100,000+ clicks

⚔️ Country Challenges

  1. Click the Challenge button (bottom nav)
  2. Choose a country to challenge and bet your clicks
  3. When accepted, both countries have 30 seconds to click fast!
  4. The winning country gets both bet amounts

🚀 Missile Attacks

  1. Launch: Click the Missile button (bottom nav)
  2. Cost: Missiles cost 50 clicks
  3. Target: Enter country code (USA, AR, JP, etc.)
  4. Attack: Click fast for 30 seconds - each click = 1 damage!
  5. Shield: After being hit, countries get a 2-hour shield

⏰ Cooldown: 30 minutes between missile launches per country

🛍️ Shop - Power-ups

Click the Shop button to buy consumable power-ups:

  • ⚡ Click Boosts: Buy +100 or +500 instant clicks
  • 🛡️ Shield: Protect yourself from missiles for 1-2 hours
  • 🚀 Missile Reset: Instantly reset your missile cooldown
  • 2️⃣ Click Multiplier: Double your clicks for 60 seconds

💰 All items are purchased with your clicks

💡 Pro Tips

  • Time your missile attacks when target countries have high click counts
  • Challenge countries with similar click counts for fair duels
  • Keep an eye on the rankings to spot potential targets
  • Golden bunbats appear randomly - don't miss them!
  • Use shop power-ups strategically to gain advantages in battles
  • Shields are useful before expected missile attacks!
